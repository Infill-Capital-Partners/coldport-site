The Opportunity

Essential Infrastructure.
Structural Tailwinds.

The average US cold storage facility is over 40 years old. Modern demand from e-commerce, pharmaceuticals, and food exports has outpaced supply. Port-adjacent capacity is critically undersupplied.

❄️

Aging Infrastructure

40+ year average facility age demands a generational upgrade. Modern requirements — automation, multi-temp zones, energy efficiency — can't be met by legacy assets.

📦

E-Commerce & Pharma

Online grocery, meal kits, and mRNA therapeutics are permanently shifting cold chain demand. Last-mile cold storage near ports is now essential infrastructure.

🏗️

Federal Funding Wave

INFRA grants, Clean Ports Program, PIDP funding — billions in federal capital de-risking private port infrastructure investment for operators who are ready.

🔒

High Barriers to Entry

$150–$300/sq ft development cost, specialized cooling, FDA/USDA compliance, and energy infrastructure requirements prevent speculative oversupply.
